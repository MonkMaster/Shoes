Privacy Policy for www.shoemonk.com

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at admin@shoemonk.com.

At www.shoemonk.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.shoemonk.com and how it is used.

Our website is supported by affiliate marketing ads, which means we get paid commission on the sale of some the products we display. Shoemonk is not influenced by advertisers or affiliate marketing programs. Our number one goal is to provide you with the best deals on shoes we can find.

Our affiliate marketing is done through SkimLinks. SkimLinks drops a cookie into a users browser when that user clicks an out bound link from our site. This cookie is used to read anonymous information about the pages, links, and merchants visited by the user, so SkimLinks can optimize their service. For a more detailed description of SkimLinks privacy policy you can go here. You can also opt-out of the SkimLinks cookie here.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, www.shoemonk.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
